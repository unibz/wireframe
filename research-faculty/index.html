Forschung an der Fakultät für Naturwissenschaften & Technik

The research at the Faculty of Science and Technology is characterized by an inter- to trans-disciplinary spectrum of topics related to natural environments (e.g. forests, grasslands, rivers, high-mountain spaces, agricultural land) and engineering topics concerning urban-industrial environments (e.g. industrial production realities, industrial markets, renewable energy production systems, energy efficiency solutions in buildings).

Main research areas:

  1. Agricultural Production and Food Technologies
  2. Management and Technologies for Mountain Environments
  3. Engineering methods and technologies for products and processes innovation
  4. Energy Resources and Energy Efficiency

Research in Fundamental Sciences is also carried out, covering the following areas: chemistry, mathematics and statistics.
